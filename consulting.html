DevOps
Companies today are faced with the challenges of growing their organization amidst a rapidly changing technical environment. Many firms have started adopting Agile and other new Software Development Methodologies, and leveraging new technologies like Virtualization and Cloud. This transformation, along with the business demands of faster time to market, puts Development and QA organizations under pressure to release more frequently and to take on a higher number of concurrent projects. As a result, demand on IT Operations has increased dramatically, and companies must address whether they need to update processes, implement faster tools, or bring in more specialized resources.

D2D Technologies DevOps services include solutions to respond to these needs through Software Configuration Management, Deployment Management, and Environment Management. Whether you are building out a new team to focus on this area, already working with a preferred software provider to bring in Application Lifecycle Management (ALM) solutions and more automation technologies, or seeking a new type of resource ranging from Deployment and Data Analysts to Environment Engineers, our DevOps practice offers an extensive network and years of experience hiring, training, and recruiting these specialized resources.

Our expertise in DevOps results in increased efficiency and higher development numbers through reduced downtime and production risks.
